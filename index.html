Sheaves in Geometry and Logic

Past meetings

8th meeting - 14 Feb 2017

Speaker: Jakob von Raumer (video)

Grothendieck topologies and sites. Trivial, dense and atomic topologies. Bases of Grothendieck topologies. Sheaves over a site. Limits of sheaves. Sheaves over a base

5 participants.

7th meeting - 7 Feb 2017

Speaker: Paolo Capriotti (video)

Full construction of the adjunction between presheaves and étale bundles. Sheaves as a reflective subcategory of presheaves.

8 participants.

6th meeting - 31 Jan 2017

Speaker: Paolo Capriotti (video)

Bundles, locally trivial bundles, vector bundles, covering spaces. Tangent bundle of a manifold. Sheaf of sections of a bundle. Beginning of the construction of the étale bundle of a presheaf.

5 participants.

5th meeting - 24 Jan 2017

Speaker: Paolo Capriotti (video)

Sheaves on topological spaces, with various examples and non-examples.

7 participants.

4th meeting - 17 Jan 2017

Speaker: Paolo Capriotti (video)

Subobject classifier for various other presheaf categories. General case of subobject classifier for presheaves.

9 participants.

3rd meeting - 6 Dec 2017

Speaker: Paolo Capriotti (video)

Continuous actions of infinite permutation groups. Equivalence of the two definitions of subobject classifiers. Subobject classifier for \(M\)-sets.

5 participants.

2nd meeting - 29 Nov 2017

Speaker: Paolo Capriotti (video)

Continuous \(G\)-sets, definition and first properties of subobject classifiers, subobject classifiers for \(\mathsf{Set}\), \(\mathsf{Set} \times \mathsf{Set}\) and \(G\)-sets .

9 participants.

1st meeting - 22 Nov 2017

Speaker: Paolo Capriotti

Examples of topoi: presheaves, finite sets. Limits in presheaf categories.

10 participants.

0th meeting - 15 Nov 2017

Summary of the contents of the book: sheaves, Grothendieck topoi, elementary topoi, classifying topoi, locales, logical aspects of topos theory. Some examples of presheaf categories.

5 participants.
